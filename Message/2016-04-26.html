Who are we?

We are CloudIT, aiming at developing useful platforms for institutions and organisations.

What is UBC?

With almost anyone on the android platform, UBC offers a variety of uses: Set the curriculum, Campus news, Reading the news, Express inquiry, Examination arrangements, Results inquiries, Borrow books, Study vacancy inquiries and other functions all in one, which greatly facilitate the uniiversity Students everyday life.

Why do we do it?

Because walking around offices and noticeboards to scrap off info is not as fun as it sounds. Am I right?
That's where we come in and make your college life a little a bit easier.

Do you store Library and any other passwords here?

Our app provides what we'd term as a bridge between you and the University Portals. We do not hold any passwords or any user data. The University holds this information for your authentication.
